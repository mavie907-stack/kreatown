Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Effective immediately

👋 Introduction

Welcome to KreaTown. We're committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

By using KreaTown, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile information such as username, bio, and profile photo
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe
  • Content you post, upload, or share on the platform
  • Messages sent between creators and fans
  • Communications with our support team

We also collect information automatically, such as device info, IP address, browser type, and usage data.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the KreaTown platform
  • Process payments and send payout notifications
  • Send you updates, newsletters, and platform announcements
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve your experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

🤝 Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Stripe — for secure payment processing and creator payouts
  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider
  • Cloudinary — for media storage and delivery
  • Pusher — for real-time messaging features
  • Resend — for transactional email delivery
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

⚙️ Your Rights & Choices

  • Access and download your personal data at any time
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@kreatown.com.

✉️ Contact Us

Questions? Reach out at privacy@kreatown.com.
